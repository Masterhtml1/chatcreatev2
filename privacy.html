Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our chat application.

Information We Collect

  • Nickname or username you choose to use in chat rooms
  • Chat messages and content you send through our platform
  • Room codes you create or join
  • Time and date of your chat activities
  • Reports you submit about other users or content

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the chat service
  • To monitor and enforce our community guidelines
  • To respond to user reports and moderate content
  • To improve our service and user experience
  • To communicate with you about service updates or changes

Data Storage and Security

We store your chat messages and associated data securely on our servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion.

Chat messages are retained for a limited period to enable moderation and ensure compliance with our terms of service. After this period, messages are automatically deleted from our systems.

Your Rights

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to modify or correct your information
  • Right to object to processing of your data
  • Right to data portability

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to exercise your rights regarding your personal data, please contact us at privacy@chatroomlol.created.app

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
